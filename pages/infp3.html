Sustainable Agriculture

Introduction

Sustainable agriculture is a farming practice that focuses on producing food in a way that is environmentally friendly, socially responsible, and economically viable. It aims to meet the needs of the present without compromising the ability of future generations to meet their own needs.

Key Principles

Sustainable agriculture incorporates principles such as:

  • Conservation of soil and water resources
  • Minimization of chemical inputs
  • Promotion of biodiversity
  • Support for local communities

Benefits

Sustainable agriculture offers numerous benefits, including:

  • Preservation of ecosystems
  • Enhanced soil fertility
  • Reduced environmental impact
  • Improved resilience to climate change
